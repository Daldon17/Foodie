Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

  • Personal details such as name, phone number, and email address.
  • Delivery address and location information for order fulfillment.
  • Payment details, including transaction history (processed via secure payment gateways).

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your food orders accurately.
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries.
  • To send updates, offers, and order notifications.

Sharing of Information

  • With restaurant partners to fulfill your orders.
  • With delivery partners to ensure timely delivery.
  • With legal authorities when required by law.

Data Security

  • Use of encryption to protect sensitive payment details.
  • Regular monitoring for security vulnerabilities.
  • Restricted access to personal information for authorized staff only.

Cookies and Tracking

  • Use cookies to improve app performance and user experience.
  • Track order preferences for personalized recommendations.
  • Allow users to control cookie settings in their browser/app.

Third-Party Services

  • Integration with payment gateways for secure transactions.
  • Use of mapping services for accurate location tracking.
  • Analytics tools to improve app performance.

User Rights

  • Right to access the personal information we hold.
  • Right to request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to request deletion of personal information.

Data Retention

  • Retain order history for customer service and legal purposes.
  • Retain payment records as per applicable tax laws.
  • Delete unnecessary personal data after retention period expires.

Changes to Privacy Policy

  • Notify users of major policy updates via app/email.
  • Post updated policy on the website/app with revision date.
  • Encourage users to review policy changes regularly.

Contact Us

  • Dedicated email for privacy-related concerns.
  • Customer support available for privacy queries.
  • Postal address for written communication regarding privacy matters.
